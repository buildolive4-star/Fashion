Essential Inquiries

Crucial details regarding the elite home wellness experience.

Which premium geographical areas are currently served?

Currently, OOOO services are available throughout Delhi NCR, Premium Mumbai Areas (South/West suburbs), and Bangalore. We are systematically expanding to other luxury centers.

How does the home treatment space need to be prepared?

No preparation is required on your part. Our master practitioners arrive fully prepared with portable massage tables, fresh white linens, aromatherapy oils, and mist machines. We only need a quiet, comfortable space.

What is the policy for cancellation or rescheduling?

Reservations can be safely cancelled or rescheduled up to 4 hours prior to the therapy time. Late cancellations will involve an standard booking fee.

Corporate Policies

Privacy is the cornerstone of our brand. All guest booking records, geographical addresses, and communication logs are kept under strict end-to-end security protocol. No data is shared with external groups.

All therapies booked with OOOO are strictly focused on luxury physical wellness. Practitioners maintain full authority to terminate sessions in case of any inappropriate behaviour. In such conditions, no refund will be issued.

Cancellations processed 4 hours prior to scheduled treatments will yield a complete refund back to the initial online payment method within 3-5 standard business days.
