Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your luxury experience.

Which cities do you currently serve?

Currently, OOOO premium spa services are active across Delhi NCR, Mumbai, and Bangalore. We plan to expand to other luxury cities soon.

Do I need to prepare anything at home before therapist arrival?

No preparation is required. Our therapists arrive with massage tables, clean linens, music therapy equipment, and essential oil blends. We only need a quiet, temperature-controlled room.

What is your cancellation and booking rescheduling policy?

You can cancel or reschedule bookings up to 4 hours before the session. Refunds for cancelled sessions will be fully credited back to your original payment method.
